Center Head's Desk


Mr. Aakash

B.Tech (Mech.), Alumni - CLIT, UK


Every year more than 10 lakh engineering aspirants appear for IIT JEE. Students put in their best efforts to secure a place in the most coveted IITs and bag a high paying job. In the year 2019, approximately 13 lakh students appeared for JEE Mains, and about 1.5 lakh qualified for JEE Advanced Exam. With the increasing number of aspirants for this highly competitive test, it is clear that the competition is going to be really tough in the years to come. We recognize the importance of offering high quality, affordable education providing a substantial foundation for students so that they can pursue their dream of getting into IIT and AIIMS, as well as making new acquaintances. At Tesla Academy, we create an environment that resonates with the learning pattern for students which leads to success in IIT JEE and Medical Exams. Our faculties are well qualified and experienced as well as giving help and assistance to students that are so necessary for strategic studies for IIT JEE and MEDICAL. Our farsightedness of creating an adaptive, learning, and testing platform for which our team is working hard to achieve the goal of getting into top-notch colleges of engineering and medical. The success of Tesla Academy is based on the success of students. We infuse in them the an attitude that does not let them settle before mounting the heights of success and deriving the best not only academically but also socially.
